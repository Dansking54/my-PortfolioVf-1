About

Who's behind the camera — and why everything looks a little golden.

I shoot the in-between.

I'm Dan — a Montréal photographer and filmmaker drawn to warm light, real faces, and the texture of ordinary places.

Whether it's a portrait, a wedding, a brand campaign or a short film, my work lives in the honest moments most people walk past. I shoot stills and motion with the same instinct: wait, watch, and press at the right second.

On the side I also do web design and flyers — but the camera always comes first.
