About

Behind the camera

I'm Dan — a photographer and filmmaker from Montréal, drawn to warm light, real faces, and the texture of ordinary places.

Whether it's a portrait, a wedding, a brand campaign or a short film, my work lives in the honest moments most people walk past. I shoot stills and motion with the same instinct: wait, watch, and press at the right second.

When I'm not behind a camera I'm designing — sites, flyers, the occasional visual identity — but the lens always comes first.
